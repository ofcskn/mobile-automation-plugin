My App helps busy professionals manage their day with AI-powered task prioritization.

Unlike traditional to-do apps, My App learns your work patterns and automatically suggests what to focus on next. Spend less time planning and more time doing.

KEY FEATURES
• AI task prioritization based on deadlines and importance
• Daily focus mode — one task at a time
• Recurring habits with streak tracking
• Calendar integration (Google, Apple)
• Offline-first — works without internet

PRIVACY
Your tasks stay on your device. No account required. No data sold.
